Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started Guide

  1. Sign up for an account as a farmer, buyer, or local market.
  2. Log in to access your personalized dashboard.
  3. Farmers: Add products, view buyer requests, and manage orders.
  4. Buyers/Markets: Search for products, place orders, and track deliveries.
  5. Use the sidebar to access your profile, settings, notifications, and more.
  6. Check the analytics and services page for market trends and tips.
  • How do I buy products? Log in as a buyer or local market and use the 'Search Products' option.
  • How do I sell products? Log in as a farmer and use the 'Add Product' option.
  • How do I change my account type? Go to Settings and select a new account type.
  • How do I contact support? Use the chatbot or see the About Us page.
